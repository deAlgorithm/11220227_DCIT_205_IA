ADMISSION REQUIREMENT

Entry Routes

The School has the following entry routes:

  • Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (SSSCE).
  • Ghana West African Seniors School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).
  • Foreign Qualifications that are internationally acceptable for admission into Universities.
  • undergraduate Entry Computer Science (Entry at Level 200 depending on the assessment of your undergraduate transcript of Level 100).

Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (SSSCE)

1. Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences

Mathematical Sciences (Actuarial Science, Biomathematics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics)

The candidate must have Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies and for the electives candidates must have Credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any other two subjects.

2. Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The candidate must have Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies and for the electives candidates must have Credit passes in any three subjects.

General Certificate of Education (GCE)

Holders of the General Certificate of Education (GCE) Advanced Level (obtained not more than three years before the time of application) must, in addition to credit passes in five (5) GCE ‘O’ Level subjects (including English, Mathematics and Integrated Science) and also have credit passes in the relevant science subjects.

International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE)/ International Baccalaureate (IB)

Holders of the IGCSE/IB must possess at least Grades A */ A-C in English and Mathematics at the Standard (SL)/’O’ Level, in addition to three Higher Level (HL-A to C)/ ‘A’ Level (A to D)/IB 2 (A to C) grades in the relevant subjects.

Graduate Entry Computer Science

The level of placement for graduate entry Computer Science holders will depend on the assessment of your undergraduate certificates or transcript in relation to the programme.

HOW TO APPLY

APPLYING FOR COMPUTER SCIENCE PROGRAMMES

EASY STEPS TO APPLY

  1. Purchase an e-voucher from any of the vendors listed here . State emphatically whether you wish to apply for undergraduate or postgraduate.
  2. Login with the form on the right hand side, using the voucher SERIAL and PIN that was provided to you.
  3. Select your mode of entry and indicate your entry type (i.e Post WASSCE/SSCE, GEM ... etc). Click submit to start applying.
  4. Fill application form and attach your passport picture. The applicant’s passport picture should be in .jpg, .jpeg, .png or .gif format and must not exceed 100KB in size. Make sure you upload a picture showing your face clearly before you submit.
  5. Clicking the SAVE AND CONTINUE LATER button will save your application for editing later.
  6. Clicking the SUBMIT AND PRINT button will submit the form and generate a reference number which will be sent to the phone number you specified. Make sure you provide correct information before submitting the form. Changes after form submission is not allowed.
  7. Click logout to leave the page. On subsequent logins you will be sent to a tracking page where you can track and print the application form you filled.
