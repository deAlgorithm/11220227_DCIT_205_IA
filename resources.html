RESOURCES

Welcome to the Resources page of the Department of Computer Science, University of Ghana, Legon.

Welcome to our comprehensive resource hub for aspiring computer scientists and tech enthusiasts! Dive into a wealth of educational materials, learning resources, and tools curated to foster your knowledge in various domains of computer science. Explore a diverse range of courses, from foundational programming concepts to cutting-edge technologies. Discover hands-on projects, career development opportunities, insightful research trends, and an active community to engage, learn, and grow your skills. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned professional, our resource page is your gateway to unlocking the world of computer science.

Short Courses Offered

Introductory Courses:

  • Introduction to Computer Science - Covers basic concepts of programming and problem-solving.
  • Data Structures and Algorithms - Focuses on fundamental data structures and algorithmic design.
  • Computer Networks - Introduction to network architecture and protocols.

Advanced Courses:

  • Machine Learning - Explores various machine learning algorithms and applications.
  • Cloud Computing - Covers cloud infrastructure and services.
  • Artificial Intelligence - Focuses on AI concepts and applications.

Learning Resources

Textbooks:

  • "Introduction to Algorithms" by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein - A comprehensive guide to algorithms and data structures.
  • "Python Crash Course" by Eric Matthes - An introduction to Python programming for beginners.

Online Courses & Tutorials:

  • Coursera - "Machine Learning" by Andrew Ng - A popular course covering machine learning basics.
  • edX - "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence" by MIT - Explores the foundations of AI.
